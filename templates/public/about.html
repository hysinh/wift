About WIFT

Advocacy

The Guild of Women in Film and Television Ireland is an advocacy organisation that supports and promotes a greater representation of women in the film and television industry both on screen and behind the camera. We are a voluntary body that is run by film and television professionals with international standing.

The creative, business, and technical divisions of the art of moving pictures are all represented in our organisation from the committee members to the membership. Our committee members are internationally accredited and the recipients of industry-recognized awards.

As an organisation, we facilitate opportunities for women in the film and television industry to promote their work and to network with other women and professionals in the industry.
