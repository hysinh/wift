WIFT Fellowships

Build Your Skills

WIFT-Ireland is committed to promoting women and advancing the careers of women, non-binary, and trans people in the entertainment industry

Our WIFT Fellowship program for 2025 welcomes Fellows from each sector of the entertainment industry. A WIFT Fellowship includes a year of mentoring, master classes, networking, and one-to-one career sessions as well as peer-to-peer support in our unique framework.

As a WIFT Fellow, you will have the opportunity to interact with senior industry decision-makers at our Partner Companies with opportunities for early career advancement.
